Co-creator of IN-FLUX and Color Zoom 2015 International Partner Gold Winner

Every year, Goldwell hosts an international hair coloring competition and each country sends representatives to compete on behalf of their nation—basically the hair coloring version of the Olympics. Corinne represented the USA and proudly took home gold in 2015. Following her victory she joined the international Color Zoom team and co-created Goldwell's 2017 Color Zoom trend IN-FLUX. Since then she's been a member of the Goldwell USA Artist team, her work has been featured in multiple publications, and she was named the #1 up-and-coming hair colorist under 30 by the Colorist Magazine.
